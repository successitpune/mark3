Journey Since 1999

Impetus is proud to partner with global leaders in IT education.

  • Sole Authorized Training partner for Oracle Corporation from 2011 to 2016 for PCMC region
  • Authorized Training and Certification centre for Autodesk since 2011 for most of their products (AutoCAD, Revit, Inventor, Fusion 360, 3DMax etc.)
  • Authorized training partner for PTC to deliver training of their flagship product “CREO”
  • Authorised Training Partner for Tally, the leading accounting software in India.
  • First Training Institute in Pune to be authorised by Pearson for conducting International Certification programs for C and C++. We are Pearson CPP Institute.
  • Only Pearson Testing Centre in PCMC to deliver exams and provide highest levels of service to our clients and their candidates.
  • Nodal agency of Maharashtra State Skill Development Society (MSSDS) for implementation of various skill development programmes promoted by the State government.
